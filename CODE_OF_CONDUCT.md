# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We expect all participants to treat each other with respect and create an inclusive environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome people of all backgrounds and experiences
- Be patient and understanding with those learning
- Give credit and acknowledge others' work
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Be open to different opinions and viewpoints

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable:

- Harassment, discrimination, or insults based on any protected characteristic
- Personal attacks or trolling
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Intimidation or threats
- Spam or promotional content
- Any other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Consequences

Unacceptable behavior will not be tolerated. Anyone who violates this code of conduct may be:

- Asked to stop the behavior immediately
- Removed from discussions or pull requests
- Blocked from the repository or community spaces
- Reported to the appropriate authorities if warranted

## Reporting

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it to:

**Email:** abuse@tryrepath.com

Include:
- Description of the incident
- Who was involved
- When and where it occurred
- Any relevant context or links

All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially and fairly.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:

- All project repositories and issue trackers
- Pull request discussions and code reviews
- Community forums and discussions
- Official events and meetups
- Any official Repath communication channels
